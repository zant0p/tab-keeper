# Tab Keeper - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 9, 2026

## Data Collection & Storage

Tab Keeper stores the following data **locally on your device** using Chrome's storage API:

### What We Store
- **Target URL**: The URL you want to keep active
- **Return Timer**: Your preferred inactivity timeout (1-60 minutes)
- **Username & Password**: Auto-login credentials (if configured)
- **Extension Settings**: Enable/disable status and preferences

### What We DO NOT Collect
- ❌ No data is transmitted to external servers
- ❌ No browsing history is collected or stored
- ❌ No analytics or tracking
- ❌ No data is synced to Google accounts
- ❌ No third-party services have access to your data

## Data Security

**Local Storage Only:**
- All data is stored in Chrome's local storage API
- Data is accessible only by the Tab Keeper extension
- Storage is tied to your Chrome profile
- Data is **not encrypted** (Chrome storage is plain text)

**Security Recommendations:**
1. Use a dedicated Chrome profile for kiosk/monitoring use
2. Do not use with highly sensitive accounts (banking, etc.)
3. Clear credentials when no longer needed
4. Use strong, unique passwords for monitored accounts

## Data Usage

The extension uses stored data solely for:
- Monitoring tab activity and switching back to target URL
- Auto-filling login credentials when login pages are detected
- Maintaining your preferred settings

## Third-Party Services

Tab Keeper does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs. All functionality runs locally within your browser.

## Children's Privacy

Tab Keeper is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date will reflect any changes.
